Andrew Parks is... a content strategist who took a recent detour into UX design.

Over the past 18 years, he has worked for Apple, Rolling Stone, Entertainment Weekly, and eMusic; produced owned media projects for Marriott International, Red Bull, Christie’s, and Hennesy; and overseen an online music magazine (self-titled) and local arts & culture outlet (Minnevangelist). He is currently leading a complete overhaul of Songtrust's in-house editorial and customer service copy.
