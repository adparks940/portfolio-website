The Task

As part of a special electronic music package I spearheaded for self-titled, I oversaw an owned media campaign featuring the major equipment brand Moog Music and three underground producers (Gavin Rayna Russom, Laurel Halo, Brenmar).

The Results

The project's free digital EP generated more than 70,000 streams on SoundCloud and raised customer awareness of the company's new Sub Phatty synth in the months leading up to a subsequent campaign featuring Adult Swim and Flying Lotus.
