The 411

Over the past 18 years, I've worked for Apple, Rolling Stone, Entertainment Weekly, eMusic, and Songtrust; edited travel guides for Michelin and Fodor's; written liner notes for Pantera and Ghostly International; and produced owned media content for such major brands as Marriott International, Red Bull, Christie's, LVMH Moët Hennessy, Activision, Bandcamp, Visit California, and Explore Minnesota.

A writer, editor, and researcher since the early days of Myspace (miss you, Tom), I filed my first cover story on Kanye West and have reported on everything from China's billion-dollar baijiu industry to Tazmania's edgy art scene. When I'm not busy contributing to such publications as Food & Wine, Bon Appétit, Condé Nast Traveler, Entrepreneur, AFAR, Travel + Leisure, and New York Magazine, I publish a leading online magazine about underground music (self-titled) and oversee the creative direction of an arts & culture site centered in the Twin Cities (Minnevangelist).

After taking a slight detour into UX design over the past year, I'm now looking to bridge the gap between traditional journalism and tech-savvy storytelling. After all, content isn't the only thing that's king these days; equally important (maybe even more so) are the people who actually consume it.
